Work

Understanding Your Needs:
We begin by getting to know you—your purpose, budget, preferred location, and any specific requirements related to layout, lifestyle, legal clarity, or Vastu alignment. This helps us offer solutions that truly match your goals.

Property Shortlisting & Site Visits:
Based on your brief, we handpick properties that meet your expectations. We arrange site visits and offer unbiased comparisons based on location, builder reputation, resale value, and development potential.

Legal Due Diligence:
Every shortlisted property is thoroughly verified by our legal expert. We check title clarity, ownership documents, RERA approvals, and ensure all legal parameters are met before proceeding.

Vastu Shastra Guidance:
Our in-house Vastu expert evaluates the shortlisted properties to help you choose a home aligned with peace, prosperity, and positive energy. We also advise simple layout corrections when needed.

Negotiation & Documentation:
We assist you in negotiating the best price, drafting agreements, completing registration formalities, and ensuring a smooth documentation process at every stage.

Loan & Financial Support:
We connect you with trusted home loan partners, help prepare your loan file, and coordinate sanction and disbursement. We also guide you on eligible subsidy schemes and tax benefits.

Possession & Beyond:
At the time of possession, we ensure all commitments are met by the builder or seller. We continue to support you with utility transfers, society formation, and recommendations for interiors or renting.

With Naume Realty Solutions, you're choosing expert guidance, legal peace of mind, and a home that’s energetically and emotionally aligned with your future.
